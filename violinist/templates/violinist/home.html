About

I am a graduating senior at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.

I have been accepted into the St. Mary's University School of Law in San Antonio, TX.

My hobbies include music (violin and flamenco guitar), football, and tennis.


Musician

My musical career began at age 5 with the Suzuki Program. After many hours of "practice and practice like crazy," I began taking private lessons.

The teachers who had the largest impact on my musical career were Guyane Grigorian and Dr. Jorge Richter. Their teachings fueled my love for violin and music as a whole.

As my passion for music increased, so did the quality of my play. In high school, I was concert master for the North Carolina State Orchestra, participated in the Eastern Youth Orchestra, and ultimately earned a scholarship to perform at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.

Sports

I played 4 years of high school tennis at Arendell Parrott Academy in Kinston, NC. My tennis career did not stop in high school, however. I maintain an active tennis practice schedule, teach lessons, and was a North Carolina State Combo Doubles Champion at the 8.5 level.

For me, football is life. Whether preseason, postseason, draft, or combine, you will never see me 'out of the loop' when talking about the NFL. I am a lifelong Pittsburgh Steelers fan, much to the chagrin of my other family members.
